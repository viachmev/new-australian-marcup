Day 1 - Hamilton Island

Arrive Sydney Airport– Hotel Transfer – Walk to Sydney Opera House – Take Opera House Tour – Explore the Rocks Historic Precinct

Explore the World Heritage listed Blue Mountains National Park. View the famous Three Sisters rock formation from a perfect vantage point before arriving at Scenic World, where you’ll witness the vastness of the Blue Mountains. Stop in picturesque Leura where you'll enjoy lunch in a local café. There’s time to explore the village, a delightful blend of quaint shops and groomed gardens.
